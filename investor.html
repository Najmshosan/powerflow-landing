Investor terms summary

  • The investor is the owner of the qualifying asset purchased from Trefcel Energy, whether bought outright or on approved instalment.
  • Ownership is governed by signed platform and asset-use agreements.
  • Trefcel Energy may manage installation, monitoring, metering and consumer allocation under agreed operating terms.
  • Investors may sell excess power to connected consumers where technically and contractually approved.
  • Settlement, maintenance, instalment default handling, operating limits and revenue rights should be governed by the signed investor agreement.
