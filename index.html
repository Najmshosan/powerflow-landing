How the model works

Trefcel Solar installs and operates the platform. Investors own the solar products or power assets they purchase from us. Consumers subscribe, receive smart-metered access, and can buy electricity from the network.

  • Investor buys a solar asset outright or on instalment
  • Trefcel deploys, monitors and meters the asset
  • Consumer uses electricity through a smart meter account
  • Excess energy can be sold back into the network to approved consumers

Registration journey

Account registration for consumers and investors

Your account registration page should clearly ask users to choose whether they are joining as a Consumer or an Investor, because the journey, products, ownership structure and commercial rights are different.

Consumer Registration Page Should Capture

  • Full name, phone, email and address
  • Property type: home, office, shop, estate, hostel or commercial premises
  • Expected usage and appliance profile
  • Need for AC or inverter AC usage support
  • Electrician and site wiring readiness
  • Agreement acceptance before payment
  • Meter request and installation workflow

Investor Registration Page Should Capture

  • Full name, phone, email, address and KYC details
  • Preferred investment type: outright purchase or instalment
  • Preferred asset type: home solar, commercial solar, EV charging asset, mini-grid asset
  • Expected ownership size and deployment location
  • Whether asset output should power own site, third-party consumers, or both
  • Acceptance of ownership, maintenance, revenue and resale terms
  • Agreement to platform settlement and asset-use rules

Installation, ownership and investor terms

These terms should appear clearly on your registration page and before payment.

Consumer Terms

  • Consumers can still power essential loads and AC usage, with inverter AC preferred for efficiency.
  • A registration fee applies before meter onboarding and activation.
  • The customer is responsible for wiring from the main line to the premises.
  • The customer agrees directly with the electrician on workmanship and labour cost.
  • Trefcel Solar provides the smart meter, changeover switch and electrical breaker free under service terms.
  • These supplied items remain the property of Trefcel Solar.
  • Faulty supplied items are replaced free where there is no customer damage or tampering.
  • The solar customer agreement must be accepted before payment.

Investor Terms

  • The investor is the owner of the qualifying solar asset purchased from Trefcel Solar, whether bought outright or under an approved instalment plan.
  • Investor ownership is subject to Trefcel Solar platform documentation, deployment rules and settlement terms.
  • Trefcel Solar may manage installation, smart metering, monitoring, consumer allocation and technical operations on the owner’s behalf.
  • Where permitted, investors may sell excess energy to connected consumers through the Trefcel Solar platform.
  • Settlement, maintenance scope, uptime, tampering, relocation, repossession on default, and asset control terms should be expressly documented in the investor agreement.
Important: Damage, tampering, bypassing, unauthorised rewiring, meter interference, or misuse of supplied equipment may void replacement support, cause service suspension, and trigger recovery or enforcement actions under the signed agreement.
