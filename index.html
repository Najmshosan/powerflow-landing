Terms, installation and ownership conditions

These terms should appear before checkout and before the account is fully activated.

Consumer Terms

  • Consumers can still power appliances including AC, with inverter AC preferred for efficiency.
  • A registration fee applies before activation.
  • The customer is responsible for wiring from the main line to the office or premises.
  • The customer agrees electrician workmanship and labour cost directly with the electrician.
  • Trefcel Energy provides the smart meter, changeover switch and electrical breaker free under service terms.
  • These supplied items remain the property of Trefcel Energy.
  • Replacement is free for qualifying faults where there is no customer damage or tampering.
  • The solar customer agreement must be signed before payment.

Investor Terms

  • The investor is the owner of the qualifying asset purchased from Trefcel Energy, whether bought outright or on approved instalment.
  • Ownership is subject to signed platform and asset-use agreements.
  • Trefcel Energy may manage installation, metering, monitoring and consumer allocation under agreed operating terms.
  • Investors may sell excess power to connected consumers where technically and contractually approved.
  • Maintenance, settlement, repossession on default, operating limits and revenue rights must be governed by the signed investor agreement.
Damage, tampering, bypassing, meter interference, unauthorised rewiring or misuse of supplied equipment may void replacement support and may result in suspension, enforcement action or recovery under the signed agreement.
