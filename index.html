Installation Terms & Conditions

Registration for a Trefcel Solar smart meter costs ₦100,000. Customers handle wiring from their main supply to their building and arrange workmanship fees with their electrician.

Trefcel Solar provides the smart meter, changeover switch, and electrical breaker free of charge. These remain Trefcel's property and are replaced free if they fail from normal use — damage or tampering voids replacement coverage.

All customers must review and sign the Solar Customer Agreement before making payment.
